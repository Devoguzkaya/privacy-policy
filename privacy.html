Privacy Policy

This extension does not collect, store, track, or share any kind of personal data. All settings and preferences are stored locally on your own device using Chrome's internal storage system. No information is ever sent to any server, third party, or developer.

This extension was created by a developer who is just starting their journey in the world of software development. It is completely free to use, and no payment, subscription, or donation is required or expected.

There are no advertisements, no tracking mechanisms, and absolutely no hidden functionality. The entire purpose of this extension is to give users a cleaner YouTube experience by allowing them to hide Shorts content if they choose to do so.

The project was developed as a personal learning experience and shared publicly in the hope that it might also be helpful to others.

For any questions, feedback, or suggestions, you can contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store contact email.
